IK History

Founded in 1936 by Maulana Muhammad Shafi Khan, the Islamia Karimia Society of Indore has been pivotal in Islamic education and community service. Initially focusing on Islamic learning, it expanded to serve broader educational needs. Its network includes schools, colleges, and religious centers, fostering holistic development for students of diverse backgrounds.

Throughout its history, the Islamia Karimia Society has played a pivotal role in promoting religious tolerance, social welfare, and cultural preservation. It has been instrumental in fostering harmony among diverse communities and empowering marginalized groups through education and advocacy.

Our Philosophy

We prioritize holistic education, fostering critical thinking, creativity, and ethical values. It should aim to empower students to become lifelong learners, responsible citizens, and leaders who contribute positively to society.

Our Principle

Our core principle is prioritizing students' needs and well-being. We offer comprehensive support: academic advising, counseling, career guidance, diverse extracurricular activities, nurturing varied interests and talents.

Key Of Success

Quality education, reputation, and support drive college success in India. Industry partnerships, innovation, community engagement are crucial. Effective leadership, adaptability ensure sustainability, growth.
